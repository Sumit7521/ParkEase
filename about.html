About Us

Introducing ParkEase, a software designed to cater for all car or truck or Motorbikes parking requirement.

Problem Statement

Parking challenges or problem of Urban areas often face a shortage of available parking spaces due to high population density and limited land resources.Existing parking spaces may not be used efficiently, leading to congestion and difficulties in finding available spots.Illegally parked vehicles contribute to congestion, impede traffic flow, and create safety hazards.oping and maintaining parking infrastructure can be expensive, especially in densely populated urban areas with limited space.

Our Solution

Our solution is to develop an mobile application for users to access real-time parking availability information nearest to them.

United Nations SDGs we are Addressing

  • Affordable : Our goal is to simplify the process of parking vehicles, with the aim of promoting greater use of these vehicles
  • Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure: Moreover we want to address the lack of infrastructure in the Parking and we want to make the infrastructue more organised.
